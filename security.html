How to Protect Your Account

1

Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Never reuse passwords across sites. Use a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

2

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your online banking account with 2FA via SMS or authenticator app.

3

Monitor Your Accounts Regularly

Review your transactions weekly using the Narmer Mobile app and report any suspicious activity immediately.

4

Beware of Phishing Scams

Narmer will never ask for your password or PIN via email, text, or phone. When in doubt, call us directly at 1-800-NARMER.
