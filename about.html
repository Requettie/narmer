About Us

125 Years of Financial Leadership

Founded in 1897, Narmer Financial has grown from a single storefront in Boston to one of the largest and most trusted financial institutions in the United States.

📈

Our Mission

To empower individuals, families, and businesses to achieve financial prosperity through trust, innovation, and personalized service.

👍

Our Values

Integrity, accountability, community, and excellence drive every decision we make and every relationship we build.

🌎

Our Impact

$2.5B invested in community development, 1.2M hours of employee volunteer service, and a commitment to net-zero operations by 2040.

Our History

The Narmer Timeline

1897

The Beginning

Alistair Narmer opens the Boston Savings & Trust Company in a small storefront on State Street with $15,000 in capital.

1929

The Great Depression

While hundreds of banks failed, Narmer remained solvent by maintaining conservative lending practices and depositor confidence.

1972

National Expansion

Narmer goes public on the NYSE and begins its expansion across the Eastern Seaboard, opening 200 branches in 15 states.

2004

Digital Revolution

Launch of Narmer Online Banking, becoming one of the first major banks to offer full digital account management.

2026

Today & Beyond

Serving 15 million customers across 4,200 branches with $220B in assets, Narmer continues to lead with AI-driven banking and wealth management innovation.
